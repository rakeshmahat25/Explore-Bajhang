Top Destinations

Chainpur Bazzar

Chainpur Bazar is a vibrant market town located in the Bajhang district of Nepal. It is a central hub for local trade and cultural activities in this remote area. The bazar is known for its bustling markets, traditional Nepalese atmosphere, and the friendly spirit of its residents.

Interesting Facts:

  • Local Market: Chainpur Bazar is a key place for shopping, where you can find everything from fresh fruits and vegetables to handmade crafts and clothing.
  • Cultural Hub: The bazar showcases traditional Nepalese culture through its lively market scenes and local festivals.
  • Beautiful Scenery: The town is surrounded by stunning landscapes, including hills and terraced fields, offering picturesque views and a serene environment.
  • Historical Importance: Chainpur Bazar has been a significant center of trade and culture for many years in the Bajhang district.
  • Delicious Food: Visitors can enjoy a variety of local dishes and snacks, reflecting the rich culinary traditions of the region.
Surma Sarovar

Surma Sarobar is a beautiful lake located in the Surma Valley of Bajhang district in Nepal. This serene lake is surrounded by green hills and mountains, making it a peaceful spot for visitors. It’s a perfect place for relaxation and enjoying nature.

Interesting Facts:

  • Scenic Beauty: The lake is known for its stunning views, with clear water reflecting the surrounding hills and mountains.
  • Peaceful Retreat: Because it’s in a remote area, Surma Sarobar is less crowded, offering a quiet escape from city life.
  • Local Spot: It’s a favorite spot for locals, who come here for picnics and to enjoy the natural beauty of their region.
  • Wildlife: The area around the lake is home to various birds and wildlife, making it a great place for nature enthusiasts.
  • Hidden Gem: Surma Sarobar is not as well-known as other tourist spots, which adds to its charm and makes it a special place for those who discover it.
Khaptad National Park

Khaptad National Park is a stunning protected area located in the Far-Western region of Nepal. Established in 1984, it is known for its rich biodiversity, lush landscapes, and serene beauty. The park covers an area of around 225 square kilometers and is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including rare and endangered species.

Interesting Facts:

  • Diverse Wildlife: The park is home to various animals such as the Himalayan tahr, leopard, and red panda, as well as numerous bird species.
  • Scenic Beauty: Khaptad National Park offers breathtaking views of rolling hills, meadows, and forested areas, making it a great spot for nature lovers and photographers.
  • Unique Flora: The park features a variety of plant species, including medicinal herbs and beautiful wildflowers, adding to its ecological significance.
  • Cultural Significance: The park is named after Khaptad Baba, a revered figure who meditated in the area. It is a place of spiritual importance for many local people.
  • Adventure Opportunities: Visitors can enjoy activities such as trekking, wildlife viewing, and camping while exploring the park's natural beauty.
Baril Jharana

Baril Jharana is a stunning waterfall nestled in the picturesque Bajhang district of Nepal. Known for its breathtaking natural beauty, this cascading waterfall is a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and travelers seeking tranquility away from the hustle and bustle of city life. The pristine environment and serene surroundings make it a perfect spot for relaxation and adventure.

Interesting Facts:

  • Height: Baril Jharana boasts an impressive height of approximately 80 meters, creating a spectacular view as the water plunges into the pool below.
  • Location: Situated in the Bajhang district, it is accessible via a scenic trek through lush green landscapes, adding to the allure of the journey.
  • Local Significance: The waterfall holds cultural significance for the local communities, often being featured in traditional stories and local folklore.
Saipal Base Camp

Saipal Base Camp, located in the remote Bajhang district of Nepal, is a breathtaking destination for trekkers and adventurers. Situated at the foot of the majestic Saipal Mountain, this base camp offers stunning views of the snow-capped peaks and an opportunity to immerse oneself in the pristine Himalayan environment. The journey to Saipal Base Camp is a memorable adventure through diverse landscapes, including lush forests, high-altitude meadows, and serene mountain lakes.

Interesting Facts:

  • Elevation: Saipal Base Camp sits at an altitude of approximately 4,000 meters (13,123 feet), offering spectacular panoramic views of the Saipal Mountain range.
  • Remote Location: The base camp is located in a remote area of the Bajhang district, making it a perfect destination for those seeking off-the-beaten-path trekking experiences away from the crowds.
  • Cultural Significance: The trek to Saipal Base Camp passes through traditional villages where trekkers can experience the rich culture and lifestyle of the local communities, including unique customs and practices.
Bithhad Valley

Bithhad Valley is a captivating and relatively untouched destination located in the Bajhang district of Nepal. Known for its stunning natural landscapes and serene atmosphere, the valley offers an authentic experience of the Himalayan wilderness. Surrounded by lush green hills, terraced fields, and traditional villages, Bithhad Valley is a perfect spot for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty away from the more frequented tourist spots.

Interesting Facts:

  • Scenic Landscapes: Bithhad Valley is renowned for its picturesque scenery, including rolling hills, verdant terraces, and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, making it ideal for hiking and nature photography.
  • Cultural Experience: The valley is home to local communities with rich cultural traditions. Visitors can experience the traditional lifestyle, customs, and festivals of the indigenous people of the region.
  • Remote Charm: Due to its remote location, Bithhad Valley remains relatively untouched by mass tourism, providing a unique opportunity to explore a pristine environment and enjoy a peaceful retreat.
